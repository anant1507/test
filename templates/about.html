About Eco-Rakshak

Eco-Rakshak is an innovative waste management system that uses AI to automatically classify and sort waste into appropriate bins.

Our mission is to make waste management more efficient and environmentally friendly by leveraging cutting-edge technology.

The system was developed by Team ETHIX as part of our commitment to sustainable technology solutions.

Key Features:

  • Real-time waste detection and classification
  • Automated bin fill-level monitoring
  • Instant notifications when bins are full
  • User-friendly interface with camera controls
